About Plantelligence

Plantelligence is an AI-powered crop disease detection system built for farmers in Andhra Pradesh. By combining machine learning with agricultural expertise, we help farmers identify diseases early, reduce crop loss, and access smart farming guidance — all in one place.

Our system supports 7 major crops including Cotton, Rice, Chilli, Tomato, Potato, Wheat, and Mango, covering dozens of disease conditions with treatment recommendations and preventive care guides.
