🌱 Our Project Features

Everything you need for smarter, healthier, and more productive farming

⭐ Core Features

🌿

Disease Detection

Upload a diseased plant image and our AI system will instantly identify the problem.

  • Identify the crop name
  • Detect the disease accurately
  • Show all visible symptoms
  • Provide treatment methods
  • Suggest prevention techniques
🪡

Supported Crops

The system currently supports 7 major crops with multiple disease records per crop.

  • 🍅 Tomato
  • 🥔 Potato
  • 🍚 Rice
  • 🌾 Wheat
  • 🌶️ Chilli
  • 🪡 Cotton
  • 🥭 Mango
📋

Complete Plant Information

After detection, the system also shows detailed information about the crop.

  • Scientific name
  • Growth duration (days/months)
  • Suitable growing season
  • Recommended soil type
  • Water requirements
  • Common uses of the crop
🚜

Smart Agricultural Guidance

Farmers receive proper guidance on managing their crops through every stage.

  • Fertilizer suggestions (NPK)
  • Pesticide recommendations
  • Organic treatment methods
  • Preventive crop management
  • Seasonal farming calendar
